# Eight AI Forces — Image Generation Prompt

Please generate a new image.

## Critical Rule

**This image must contain absolutely no text, labels, titles, captions, numbers, arrows-with-words, callout lines, or annotation marks of any kind.** Every label, title, and number is added on top of the image as an interactive HTML overlay from a separate data file. Any text baked into the artwork will collide with the overlay text and produce a duplicated, unreadable interface.

If your image generator is tempted to add a heading like "Eight AI Forces", a center label like "AI Model Capability", or row labels like "R1", "B2", or "Recursive Self-Improvement" — **do not.** The icons must stand on their own as wordless visual symbols.

---

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG
- **Dimensions**: 1400 × 900 px (landscape, ~14:9 ratio)
- **Background**: Very light off-white / cream (#FBFAF7) with an extremely faint hexagonal or thin-line circuit pattern (no more than 4% darker than the background — should feel like a watermark, never compete with foreground)
- **Style**: Clean, modern infographic illustration. Flat-vector aesthetic with subtle gradients and soft shadows. Restrained palette, generous whitespace, professional but friendly. Suitable for a business/strategy textbook.

---

## What to Draw

A symmetric infographic poster with a central circular medallion flanked by two vertical columns of icon badges — four on the left, four on the right. The whole composition should read at a glance as "four warm-colored symbols on the left, central robot, four cool-colored symbols on the right."

**Critical empty zones (leave clear so overlay text can be placed there with no collision):**

- **Top 12% of the canvas** — completely empty cream background. The overlay system writes the title here.
- **Strip from y=33% to y=42%, centered around the medallion** — completely empty. The overlay writes the central "AI Model Capability" text here.
- **Strip immediately to the right of each left-side icon (x=18% to x=46%) at the row's y-center** — completely empty. The overlay writes that force's label here.
- **Strip immediately to the left of each right-side icon (x=54% to x=82%) at the row's y-center** — completely empty. The overlay writes that force's label here.

These four kinds of empty zones must contain **only background color and the very faint watermark pattern** — no shapes, no decorative dots, no light boxes, nothing.

## Layout Notes (please re-read before rendering)

- The composition is strongly symmetric. Left and right columns must mirror each other vertically: rows at y = 24%, 40%, 56%, 72%.
- **No text anywhere in the image.** Not in the icons. Not below the icons. Not above the medallion. Not in the title bar. Not as a footer or watermark or signature. **None.**
- No numbers in the badges. The overlay system places the numbers 1–8 on top of the icons.
- The four "empty zones" listed above (top 12%, strip around medallion, strips next to each icon) must be untouched cream background — no decorative dots, no faint shapes, no spurious lines.
- Maintain at least 80 px of clear background around every icon badge.
- The central medallion is the visual anchor — it should be roughly 30–35% larger in diameter than any of the eight icon badges.
- Background watermark pattern (faint hex / thin circuit lines) should never come within 60 px of any icon, the medallion, or the four reserved empty zones.
- No other decorative elements: no random gears, no brain illustrations, no neural-network graphics, no city skylines, no hand-drawn flourishes, no abstract swooshes, no gradient overlays across the whole canvas. Only: cream background + faint watermark + 8 icon badges + 8 dashed leader lines + 1 central medallion with robot.
- No source line, no signature, no logo, no caption. Nothing.
